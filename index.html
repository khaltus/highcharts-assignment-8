Reported Crime at Florida's Largest Universities

By Kristen Altus

Source: "https://ucr.fbi.gov/crime-in-the-u.s/2018/crime-in-the-u.s.-2018/topic-pages/tables/table-6"

Deciding where to attend college can be a big deal. Each university offers a different campus, program and student life. Another important factor to take into consideration when choosing a university is its crime rate. The graphs here look at the top five most populated universities in Florida, and the number of reported violent and property crimes in that college's city.
